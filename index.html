What is Strategy Share?

Strategy Share is a platform designed to help job seekers connect and share their interview experiences. By sharing insights and stories, users can prepare better for their interviews and support one another in their job search journey.

Why Share Your Experience?

  • Build a supportive community.
  • Help others prepare better for interviews.
  • Gain insights into various interview processes.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals.

How It Works

  1. Share your interview experience.
  2. Read others' experiences.
  3. Engage and connect with the community.

Upcoming Features

  • Roadmaps for interview preparation.
  • Job and internship postings to help you find opportunities.
  • Connect with people via email, LinkedIn, and other platforms.
